BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) to assess your body weight relative to height.

Understanding BMI:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

How to Calculate:

  1. Enter your height (in cm or inches)
  2. Enter your weight (in kg or pounds)
  3. Click calculate to see your BMI
  4. Review the health category and recommendations
