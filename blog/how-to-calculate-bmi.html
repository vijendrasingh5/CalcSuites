How to Calculate BMI: A Complete Guide

Published: February 18, 2025 5 min read
Understanding your Body Mass Index (BMI) is crucial for assessing your overall health and maintaining a healthy weight. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about BMI calculation.

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement using your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. The BMI calculation divides an adult's weight in kilograms by their height in metres squared.

The BMI Formula

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²)

Or in imperial units:

BMI = (weight (lbs) × 703) ÷ height² (inches²)

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese

How to Calculate Your BMI

  1. Step 1: Gather Your Measurements

    You'll need:

    • Your weight in kilograms or pounds
    • Your height in meters or inches

  2. Step 2: Convert Units if Necessary

    If using imperial measurements:

    • Convert weight to pounds
    • Convert height to inches

Understanding Your BMI Results

While BMI is a useful starting point for understanding your healthy weight range, it's important to remember that it's just one indicator of health. Other factors to consider include:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Muscle mass
  • Ethnic background
  • Body composition

Tips for Maintaining a Healthy BMI

  • Maintain a balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Stay hydrated
  • Monitor your weight regularly
